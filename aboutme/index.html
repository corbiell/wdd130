About Me

Ellison Corbin

Hello everyone, let me introduce myself. I immigrated from Barbados to North America over 50 years ago. I have been living in Canada, just north of Toronto, for the past 47 years. I am married with two children and two grandchildren.

I have been a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints for 45 years. I love the church, it has allowed me to become a better disciple of Jesus Christ, having Him as the center of my life.

I am retired; however, I believe in continued education. This is why I am taking this course. I worked previously as a Desktop Technician providing End User Support. I am hoping to make Web Developing my new technical venture.
